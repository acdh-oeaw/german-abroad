The MinGLA series serves as a platform for research and international exchange on Germanic-speaking minorities in Latin America. It brings together linguistic perspectives from Romance and Germanic studies (including Scandinavian, Dutch, and Yiddish studies) while also welcoming contributions from related disciplines in the humanities and from transdisciplinary approaches.
