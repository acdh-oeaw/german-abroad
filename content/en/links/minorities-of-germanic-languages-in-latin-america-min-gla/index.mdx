The MinGLA network was established in 2019 during the Eichstätt conference *“*German and Other Germanic Minority Languages in Latin America: Methods, Fundamentals, Case Studies” ('Deutsche und weitere germanische Sprachminderheiten in Lateinamerika: Methoden, Grundlagen, Fallstudien'). It aims to connect research on minority Germanic languages in Latin America and is open to anyone working in this field.
