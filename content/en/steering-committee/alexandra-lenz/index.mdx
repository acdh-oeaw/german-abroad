... has been Director of ACDH-CH since 2020 (having previously served as Vice Director) and full professor of German linguistics at the University of Vienna since 2010. As Director of the ACDH-CH, she is responsible for the research institute as a whole, which consists of four research units ([DH Research\&Infrastructure](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research-units/dh-researchinfrastructure), [Linguistics](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research-units/linguistics), [Literary\&Textual Studies](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research-units/literarytextual-studies) and [Musicology](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research-units/musicology)).

At ACDH-CH, she also heads the research unit [Linguistics](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research-units/linguistics), whose goal is to bundle all linguistic activities of ACDH-CH and to promote and strengthen research in the context of Digital Linguistics at the ÖAW (and beyond).

The focal point of her research lies in the areas of Digital Humanities (especially Digital Linguistics), language variation (focus on syntax, lexis and phonology), language change and language history and research into language attitudes. She is speaker of the special research programme [German in Austria. Variation – Contact – Perception](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/projects/german-in-austria) (FWF F060), which began on January 1, 2016, as well as leader of three of its eight project parts.

She serves as a Full Member of the Austrian Academy of Sciences, adjunct professor at the Research Center "Deutscher Sprachatlas" (Marburg, Germany), as well as being a member of scientific advisory boards of several leading linguistic institutions and networks in Europe.
